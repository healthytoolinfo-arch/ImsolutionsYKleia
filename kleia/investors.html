Client Onboarding Flow

Clients complete a quick intake form with preferences, dietary restrictions, and goals. The system builds a personalized profile for future recommendations.

Smart Plan Generation

Kleia uses AI and templates to generate a baseline meal plan. Nutritionists can review, edit, and the system learns from their adjustments for future plans.

Client Delivery & Engagement

Clients receive their plans via the app, including editable grocery lists and daily check-ins. Progress and mood are tracked with simple inputs.

Monitoring & Signals

Automated analytics highlight adherence, trends, and early warnings. Nutritionists receive alerts for timely interventions and support.

Iteration Loop

Plans are updated regularly; the model suggests swaps and adaptive serving sizes. This loop increases personalization and client retention.
